Modern life requires continuous integration.
Here are a few things I integrate into my life alonside work and education.

Family

Becoming a mother was the most profound experience of my life. I am incredibly blessed and thankful to have created such a beautiful and loving family with my lifelong partner, my husband. Together we have two beautiful young girls who inspire us to be the best versions of ourselves.

Fitness

Physical fitness is a profound aspect of my daily life. My fitness activities keep me healthy, physically and mentally. Our family enjoys being active together and I instill this in my kids through fun activities.

Nature

Nature is sacred to me and connects me to my individual self. At any opportunity, I am running in nature or exploring the natural world with my family to reconnect to my innerself and recharge my soul.
